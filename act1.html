Computer Hardware System

- computer hardware is the physical components that a computer system requires to function. It encompasses everything with a circuit board that operates within a PC or laptop; including the motherboard, graphics card, CPU (Central Processing Unit), ventilation fans, webcam, power supply, and so on.

    The Ryzen 5 3600 is a 6-core, 12-thread processor released by AMD in 2019 as part of its 3rd Gen Ryzen lineup. It has a base clock speed of 3.6 GHz and a boost clock speed of 4.2 GHz, and is built on AMD's 7nm process technology.

    Motherboard

    ASUS PRIME H510M-E

    The Asus Prime H510M-E is a micro ATX motherboard released by Asus in 2020. It is based on the Intel H510 chipset and supports Intel 10th Gen Core processors. It has 2 memory slots, 4 SATA III ports, 1 M.2 slot, and Realtek RTL8111H Gigabit LAN. It also has support for WiFi 6 (802.11ax) through a separate WiFi card. Other features include USB 3.2 Gen 1 and 2 ports, and HD audio.

    It has a capacity of 16GB and operates at a clock speed of 2666 MHz, making it suitable for use in high-end desktop computers. DDR4 is the fourth generation of DDR (Double Data Rate) memory technology and offers improved performance, higher speed, and greater reliability compared to its predecessors.

    1tb samsung 870 qvo ssd

    It is a type of solid-state drive (SSD) released by Samsung. It has a storage capacity of 1TB and uses the SATA III interface for connecting to a computer's motherboard. The 870 QVO is part of Samsung's QVO (Samsung Quality and Value Optimized) series of SSDs, which are designed for budget-conscious users who want to upgrade their system's storage.
